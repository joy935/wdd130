Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide clear information related to the finest rafting experiences we offer. We are committed to give tailored recommendation to our visitors and to draw attention to the necessity to protect the environment. Our website will allow visitors to discover the tours and packages we offer and book and pay for the service they are interested in. Our website will be enriched with articles, photos, reviews, advices and an FAQ. Finally, visitors will be able to contact us for any question, concern or request.

Audience

For safety reasons there are certain restrictions depending on the tours we offer. The minimum age varies between 5 to 12 years old, the minimum weight varies from 20kg (45lbs) to 45kg (90lbs) and the maximum weight is 160kg (350lbs). Our target customers are young families, physically fit young adults and seniors. They are adventure-oriented and in search of an affordable, safe, spectacular and thrilling escapade. They also are interested in discovering incredible landscapes and wildlife with highly trained and experienced guides. The audience target will mainly access the site with a desktop or a laptop.
