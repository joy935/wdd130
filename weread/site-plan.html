Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the We Read website is to provide detailed and engaging book reviews. The website will offer reviews for fiction and non-fiction books. The website will serve as a platform to get people together to read more or to start to read, a book at a time.

Audience

The target audience is teenagers, young adults and adults regardless of gender. They may be avid readers or in search of a book that will start their reading journey. The teenagers and young adults audience would highly access the site with a phone and more mature audience with a desktop or a laptop.

Content

Home page

Welcome to We Read - Discover Your Next Adventure

Welcome to We Read, your go-to destination for insightful and comprehensive book reviews. Whether you are an avid reader looking for your next literary adventure or someone seeking recommendations and analysis, we have you covered. Our website is designed to provide a valuable resource for book enthusiasts, connecting readers with captivating fictional and nonfictional works. Prepare to dive into a world of words, ideas, and imagination as we explore the vast literary landscape together!

Why Choose We Read

At We Read, we understand the importance of making informed choices when it comes to selecting books. With the abundance of options available, it can be overwhelming to find a compelling read that aligns with your interests and preferences. That is where we step in to offer guidance and insights that help you make the best choices. Our team of passionate bookworms meticulously selects a collection of reviews, ensuring that our visitors have access to comprehensive and unbiased evaluations.

Discover New Worlds of Fiction

Escape reality and immerse yourself in the realms of fiction through our extensive collection of fictional book reviews. From gripping mysteries and heartwarming novels to mind-bending science fiction, we cover a wide range of genres. Our expert reviewers provide detailed analysis, exploring the storytelling, character development, and thematic elements that make each book unique. We hope you will uncover hidden gems, rediscover beloved books, and embark on new literary journeys with our recommendations.

Explore the Realms of Nonfiction

Knowledge seekers and truth enthusiasts will find solace in our reviews of nonfictional books. Whether you are passionate about history, science, or biographies, we have thoughtfully crafted reviews to satisfy your intellectual curiosity. Dig into the lives of inspiring individuals, gain new perspectives on world events, and expand your understanding of the world around you. Our reviews offer insightful commentary on the author's research, writing style, and the impact of their work on the reader.

Stay Up to Date and Accessible

In a rapidly evolving literary landscape, it is crucial to stay up to date with the latest releases. We Read keeps you in the loop by providing regular updates and engaging content. Our website is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, ensuring that visitors can easily navigate through our reviews, search for specific titles, and explore curated lists based on their interests.

Let's embark on an adenture

We Read invites you to embark on an adventure like no other. Whether you're seeking escapism in the pages of fiction or seeking knowledge from the depths of nonfiction, our website offers a collection of reviews and recommendations to cater to your every literary craving. Let us be your guide as you explore the vast world of books. Get ready to unlock new worlds, expand your horizons, and ignite your imagination at We Read.

Bad Cree by Jessica Johns

Bad Cress is a compelling collection of interconnected short stories that explores the experiences of Indigenous characters, particularly those from the Cree community. Through a mix of humor, wit, and vulnerability, Johns delves into themes of identity, relationships, and the complexities of contemporary Indigenous life. With its vivid storytelling and authentic voice, "Bad Cree" offers a powerful portrayal of Indigenous perspectives, shedding light on the challenges and resilience of the characters.
